Introduction - Certificate Transparency Browser Extension

Upgrade Your Internet With Certified Security

Certificate Transparency Browser Extension brings security and transparency to your web browsing.

With Certificate Transparency (CT) technology integrated with Celestia Blockchain, this extension helps you check and validate SSL/TLS certificates quickly and accurately.

Key Features:

Certificate Registration and Storage: Record and store SSL/TLS certificates on the blockchain to ensure transparency and security.

Certificate Validation: Check and validate the validity of certificates instantly.

Graylist Management: Monitor suspicious certificates and block potential threats.

Security Event Management: Track and log security events to keep you safe.

Decentralized management: Grant access and management of certification data to stakeholders.

Why Choose Prism CT Browser Extension?

Transparency and Security: Leverage the Celestia Blockchain to ensure absolute transparency and security for your certificates.

Easy to use: Friendly interface and easy to use for everyone.

Fast and Efficient: Process and validate certificates quickly and efficiently.
